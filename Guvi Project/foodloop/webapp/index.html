The Problem: Hunger in India

India is home to the largest number of hungry people in the world. Here are some alarming statistics:

194M

People in India are undernourished.

30%

Of the world's hungry population lives in India.

40%

Of food produced in India is wasted every year.

How FoodLoop Works

FoodLoop connects food donors with NGOs and delivery partners to ensure no food goes to waste.

1. Donate Food

Users donate excess food through the app.

2. Match with NGOs

Admins match donations with NGOs in need.

3. Deliver Food

Delivery partners pick up and deliver the food.
